🎯 项目动机

Motivation： The Challenge: In XJTLU’s international academic setting, group projects often suffer from scattered communication across fragmented platforms and information overload. While students (primary users) struggle with unequal participation and language barriers, academic teachers (secondary users) find it difficult to monitor group progress and provide timely intervention based on actual discussion quality. The Need: There is a critical need for a human-centric platform that bridges this gap. Our group chose the B1: XJTLU Group Discussions track to develop a solution that streamlines information flow for students while offering teachers visibility into the collaborative process without intrusive oversight. Our Vision: We aim to build a web-based ecosystem that integrates Generative AI to act as an intelligent facilitator. By balancing academic rigor with a "playful" experience, we seek to transform stressful teamwork into an inclusive and productive social interaction, empowering students to lead and teachers to support more effectively within the XJTLU community.
